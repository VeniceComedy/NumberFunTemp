NumberFun About

 

    NumberFun Media was founded by Derya Altay Rogers in Los Angeles, Californa. Altay, who had a successful media career of more than 10 years in her homeland country of Turkey now brings her creativity and knowledge to America. Early in her career, Altay was a Production Assistant for two of the largest Turkish media companies working on some of the most popular scripted and late night television shows in Turkey, the Middle East and Eastern Europe. In the latter part of her career she was promoted to Associate Producer and Acquisition Executive. As an AE she managed the acquisitions department overseeing the developing and executing strategies while leading negotiations and overseeing the international legal process.

    NumberFun Media's primary goal is the entertainment sector; we create comedy shows, live music, premiers and many more enjoyable nights for our target audiences. In addition to this, we create opportunities by guiding creative artistic minds such as comedians, musicians, writers and many more artists and connecting them to the right people.

    Join us to be part of the fun! Be fun or be gone!
